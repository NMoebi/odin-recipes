Pancakes

photo by Dr. Bernd Gross, CC BY-SA 3.0, via Wikimedia Commons

Description

A pancake, also known as a hotcake, griddlecake, or flapjack, is a flat type of batter bread like cake, often thin and round, prepared from a starch-based batter that may contain eggs, milk, and butter, and then cooked on a hot surface such as a griddle or frying pan. (Wikipedia)

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Butter/Oil

Steps

  1. Mix flour, milk, eggs and a pinch of salt.
  2. Throw the mix into a preheated pan with oil or butter.
  3. Once the lower side is firm and golden brown, turn it and bake the other side.
  4. Put it onto a plate, put Nutella or something else on it, and you're good to go.
