Chili con Carne

photo by kalhh (pixabay.com)

Description

Chili con carne, often shortened to chili, is a spicy stew of Mexican origin containing chili peppers (sometimes in the form of chili powder), meat (usually beef), tomatoes and often beans. Other seasonings may include garlic, onions, and cumin. (Wikipedia)

Ingredients

  • Chili peppers
  • Meat
  • Tomatoes
  • Beans
  • Corn
  • Onions
  • Salt
  • Spices
  • Oil

Steps

  1. In a pot add oil, onion, chili pepper, meat and stir until the meat is browned.
  2. Add tomatoes, salt and whatever spices you like. Cover and simmer for 45 minutes.
  3. Add beans and corn and cook for another 15 minutes.
